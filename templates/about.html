About Bookworm DB

Bookworm DB is the up and coming popular source for any and everything book, author, or publisher related. Bookworm DB was created for book lovers all around the world to explore new books and learn more about the authors and publishers. Our database contains books from a variety of generes: romance, history, non-fiction, etc. For book readers with a favorite author, Bookworm DB allows you to search an author and all the books they've written. If you want to know more about publishers, you can also search specific publishers. Use Bookworm DB to further grow and expand your book collection!
